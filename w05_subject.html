Chase Atlantic

Who are they?

Chase Atlantic is a versatile alternative pop band that originated in Cairns, Queensland. The trio formed in 2011, comprised of two bothers, Mitchel Cave and Clinton Cave, along with their friend, Christian Anthony. The band has gained prominence for its distinctive fusion of pop, rock, and R&B elements, creating a sound that defies easy genre categorization.

Meet Chase Atlantic

Mitchel Cave

Mitchel Cave, better known off as Mitty, is the lead singer of the band. Born on October 2, 1993, Mitchel serves as the lead vocalist for the trio, bringing a distinctive and soulful voice to the group's sound. His charismatic stage presence and emotive delivery contribute to the band's captivating live performances. Beyond his role in Chase Atlantic, Mitchel is known for his creative contributions to the band's songwriting, adding a personal touch to the lyrics that often explore themes of love, relationships, and self-discovery. With his unique vocal style and collaborative spirit, Mitchel Cave has become a central figure in the band's success, resonating with fans and contributing to the dynamic energy that defines Chase Atlantic's music.

Clinton Cave

Clinton Cave, born on October 21, 1992, is a versatile musician and a vital component of the Australian alternative pop band Chase Atlantic. As the guitarist, saxophonist, and programmer for the trio, Clinton contributes to the band's distinctive sound, infusing their music with a blend of rock, pop, and R&B elements. His multifaceted talents extend beyond traditional instruments, showcasing a proficiency in programming that enhances the band's electronic and experimental facets. As one of the founding members, Clinton Cave, alongside his brother Mitchel Cave and bandmate Christian Anthony, has played a pivotal role in shaping the band's identity. His musical prowess, combined with a creative and collaborative spirit, continues to be a driving force in Chase Atlantic's success, captivating audiences around the world.

Christian Anthony

Christian Anthony, born on January 5, 1995, is a key figure in the Australian alternative pop band Chase Atlantic. Serving as the guitarist and vocalist, Christian brings a dynamic and melodic dimension to the group's sound. His intricate guitar work and harmonious vocals contribute to the band's eclectic mix of genres, ranging from pop to rock and R&B. Christian, alongside his bandmates Mitchel Cave and Clinton Cave, forms the core of Chase Atlantic, contributing not only to the musical arrangements but also to the group's overall aesthetic. Albums

  • Dalliance (2014 EP)
  • Nostalgia (2015 EP)
  • Paradise (2016 EP)
  • Part Three (2017 EP)
  • Chase Atlantic (2017)
  • DONT TRY THIS (2019)
  • Phases (2019)
  • BEAUTY IN DEATH (2021)

Each of Chase Atlantic's albums have their own unique sound and feel. Dalliance and Nostalgia both showcase their 90s alternative rock sound. Paradise showcases a soft romantic pop genre. Part Three is a short EP that helps the band transition into pop and a more electronic sound. Their self titled EP, Chase Atlantic, seems to be their most popular album, showcasing their more alternative pop sound and catchy songs including Into It and Swim. DONT TRY THIS helped the band transition back to a soft melancholic sound but this time with more electronic sound. This transition to an electronic sound helped them gain popularity for Phases and BEAUTY IN DEATH, which both have rap, r&b and electronic elements.
