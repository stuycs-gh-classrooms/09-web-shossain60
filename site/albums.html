Each of Chase Atlantic's albums have their own unique sound and feel. Dalliance and Nostalgia both showcase their 90s alternative rock sound. Paradise showcases a soft romantic pop genre. Part Three is a short EP that helps the band transition into pop and a more electronic sound. Their self titled EP, Chase Atlantic, seems to be their most popular album, showcasing their more alternative pop sound and catchy songs including Into It and Swim. DONT TRY THIS helped the band transition back to a soft melancholic sound but this time with more electronic sound. This transition to an electronic sound helped them gain popularity for Phases and BEAUTY IN DEATH, which both have rap, r&b and electronic elements.
